悠译是什么？

悠译是一个跨境O2O平台，解决跨境交流合作最基本的需要：语言不通和不了解当地文化风俗。

 

为何要使用悠译？

不论消费者以何种目的出境（商务、旅行或其他），都可以随时随地在悠译平台上发布任务或搜索适合的当地翻译者——“悠译人”；与此同时，当地具有语言能力的人士，不论是专业的、兼职的或业余的翻译，都可以根据自己的语言能力选择适合的任务。

 

通过线上市场，用户们根据需要自主进行配对、轻松交易，从而打破语言和文化的壁垒，让出境更加容易。

悠译是一种生活方式，让更多想看看这个世界的人走得更远也更轻松。

悠译是一个属于世界的品牌，它让世界沟通无限。

 

 

What’s UE Lives?

UE Lives is a cross-board O2O platform, fulfilling the basic needs on communication between different languages and cultures in international cooperation.

 

Why Using UE Lives?

No matter customers go abroad for business, travel or any other purposes, they can easily publish a task or search for a suitable local interpreter—UE as we called. Meanwhile, the local who speak relevant languages, professional interpreters or amateurs, can select and apply for suitable tasks.

 

In this online market, users can match and transact based on their needs and requirements, so that the language and culture barrels can be broken down and going abroad becomes easier.

 

UE’s Dreams

UE Lives is a life-style, making people who are curious about this world can go explore the world more easily. UE Lives is a global brand, making the world communicate without barrier.
